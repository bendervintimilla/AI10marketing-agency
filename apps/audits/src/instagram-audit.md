## 2026 Instagram Algorithm Context

Instagram's 2026 ranking signals (from Meta's official transparency reports):
- **Reels** get 2-4× more distribution than static posts to non-followers
- **Original content penalty removed** (2025): reposts now distributed equally to original
- **Send rate** (shares via DM) is the #1 engagement signal for Reels reach
- **Watch-through rate**: finishing a Reel signals quality; >60% completion = positive signal
- **Carousel dwell time**: average 2.8× more time spent than single images → favored by algorithm
- **Story reply rate** matters more than story views for relationship signals
- **Hashtags (2025 update)**: follow-based hashtag feed deprecated; hashtags now function primarily as topic classifiers, not reach drivers — use 3-5 max, relevant only
- **Posting frequency sweet spot**: 4-7 Reels/week for growth accounts; 1-2 feed posts/day cap before quality signal degrades

## Content & Creative (30% weight)

| ID | Check | Severity | Pass | Warning | Fail |
|----|-------|----------|------|---------|------|
| IG-C1 | Reels adoption | Critical | ≥40% of recent posts are Reels. Reels reach 2-4× non-follower audiences vs static posts. | 20-40% Reels | <20% Reels (missing primary growth format) |
| IG-C2 | Format diversity | High | ≥3 formats active: Reels, Carousels, Stories, Feed images | 2 formats | Only 1 format (all same type) |
| IG-C3 | Carousel usage | High | ≥1 carousel/week. Avg 2.8× dwell time vs single image; saved rate 3× higher. Ideal: 5-10 slides, first slide hook. | 1-3 carousels/month | No carousels in last 30 days |
| IG-C4 | Video hook (first 3 seconds) | Critical | Strong visual hook: no black frame, motion or text on screen within 1s. Watch-through rate >60% target. | Moderate hook (logo/title card) | Slow start, fades or text-only openers |
| IG-C5 | Vertical video compliance | High | All Reels and Stories are 9:16 (1080×1920). Horizontal or square video is cropped and penalized in Reels. | Mixed ratios | Horizontal video in Reels |
| IG-C6 | Captions — Reels | High | Reels captions ≤72 chars visible in feed. Key message in first line. Include CTA ("save this", "share if"). | Caption present but over 72 chars | No caption or generic placeholder |
| IG-C7 | Hashtag strategy | Medium | 3-5 topic-relevant hashtags per post. Avoid banned/overused tags. Don't use 30-hashtag blocks (2025 deprecation). | 6-15 hashtags (pre-2025 approach) | No hashtags OR 20+ hashtag spam |
| IG-C8 | Audio / trending sound | High | ≥50% of Reels use original audio or trending audio. Sound-off Reels with captions still viable, but trending audio gets push. | Some audio use | All Reels are muted/silent content |
| IG-C9 | Content freshness | High | New Reels ≤7 days. New feed posts ≤3 days. Posting gap >14 days harms algorithmic priority. | Last post 7-14 days ago | No posts in last 14+ days |
| IG-C10 | UGC / community content | Medium | ≥20% of content features customers, behind-the-scenes, or community moments. Authenticity increases comments and shares. | 10-20% UGC | <10% (all polished/brand-only) |

## Audience & Reach (25% weight)

| ID | Check | Severity | Pass | Warning | Fail |
|----|-------|----------|------|---------|------|
| IG-A1 | Engagement rate (Feed) | Critical | ≥2.0% (followers). Industry avg 0.5-0.7%; strong brands 2-4%. Formula: (likes+comments+saves)/reach × 100 | 0.5-2.0% | <0.5% (audience disconnected) |
| IG-A2 | Reel average watch-through | High | ≥60% average watch-through on Reels. Below 30% signals low-quality content to algorithm. | 30-60% | <30% (content failing the hook test) |
| IG-A3 | Saves rate | High | ≥1% saves/reach on feed posts (especially carousels). Saves = strong "valuable content" signal. | 0.5-1.0% | <0.5% (content not seen as save-worthy) |
| IG-A4 | Story completion rate | High | ≥70% story completion rate (% who see last frame). Below 50% = content or format issue. | 50-70% | <50% (audience dropping off mid-story) |
| IG-A5 | Follower growth rate | Medium | ≥3% MoM follower growth. Flat/declining growth without Reels = under-utilizing reach formats. | 0-3% MoM | Declining followers (net loss) |
| IG-A6 | Audience geography match | High | Primary audience location matches business service area. Mis-targeted audience = low conversion rate from profile. | Partial match (>50% local) | <30% audience in target geography |
| IG-A7 | Profile visit to follow rate | Medium | ≥10% of profile visitors follow. Low rate = weak bio/value proposition. | 5-10% | <5% (bio or feed not convincing visitors) |

## Conversion & Monetization (25% weight)

| ID | Check | Severity | Pass | Warning | Fail |
|----|-------|----------|------|---------|------|
| IG-M1 | Link in bio / Linktree | High | Active link in bio pointing to a conversion destination (menu, reservation, order, website). Updated within last 30 days. | Link present but stale | No link in bio |
| IG-M2 | Bio CTA clarity | High | Bio includes a clear action: "Reserva aquí 👇", "Menú completo en el link", "WhatsApp para pedidos". | Bio has CTA but unclear | Bio is descriptive only, no action |
| IG-M3 | Stories CTA links | High | ≥50% of Stories include a link sticker or swipe-up pointing to a conversion page. | Some Stories have links | No link stickers in Stories |
| IG-M4 | Instagram Shopping / Product tags | Medium | For product-based businesses: catalog connected and products tagged in posts. For restaurants: menu highlight or "order now" link. | Partially set up | Not set up for eligible business |
| IG-M5 | Highlights organization | Medium | Story Highlights organized by customer journey: Menu / Reservas / Eventos / Reseñas / ¿Quiénes somos? | Some highlights present | No highlights OR highlights outdated >6 months |
| IG-M6 | Contact info completeness | High | Phone, email, WhatsApp, and physical address all present in profile (uses Meta Business info). | Most fields filled | No contact info or only one method |
| IG-M7 | Response rate & speed | High | Responding to DMs within 4 hours (Meta shows response badge at <1h avg). Comments responded within 24h. | DMs answered within 24h | Low/no response to DMs and comments |
| IG-M8 | Instagram Lead forms / Events | Medium | Events using "Get Tickets" or "RSVP" buttons. Reservations using "Book" action button if available. | One conversion action active | No conversion actions on profile |

## Organic + Paid Integration (20% weight)

| ID | Check | Severity | Pass | Warning | Fail |
|----|-------|----------|------|---------|------|
| IG-P1 | Top posts boosted as ads | Critical | Top 3-5 organic posts by reach/engagement are running as paid promotions. Boosting proven content = lower CPM and higher CTR vs cold creative. | Some top posts boosted | No organic posts boosted; only cold ad creative |
| IG-P2 | Partnership Ads (Branded Content) | High | At least 1 Partnership Ad (formerly Branded Content) tested with a creator or influencer account. Partnership Ads get 2× more reach than standard ads for the same budget. | Tested but not ongoing | Never tested |
| IG-P3 | Reel → Ad pipeline | High | Best-performing Reels (top 20% by reach) are repurposed as paid Reels ads within 7 days. Organic success = proven creative; paid amplification multiplies it. | Some Reels promoted | Reels never converted to ads |
| IG-P4 | Instagram-specific ad creative | Medium | Instagram ads use Instagram-native formats (9:16 Reels, 4:5 Feed). Not repurposed from Facebook Feed (wrong ratio). | Some native formats | All ads are Facebook-format repurposed to IG |
| IG-P5 | Audience from Instagram engagement | High | Custom Audience from "People who engaged with your Instagram Business profile" in the last 30-90 days used for retargeting. | Audience created but not active | No Instagram engagement audience |
| IG-P6 | Instagram placement performance tracked | Medium | Monthly review of Meta Ads Manager placement breakdown: Instagram Feed vs Stories vs Reels vs Explore. Budget reallocation based on CPM/CTR by placement. | Reviewed occasionally | Never reviewed placement breakdown |
| IG-P7 | UTM parameters on IG ad links | High | All Instagram ad links have UTM params: utm_source=instagram, utm_medium=paid_social, utm_campaign=campaign_name. GA4 correctly attributes Instagram paid traffic. | Some links have UTMs | No UTM parameters on Instagram ad links |

## Instagram Benchmarks 2026 (Food & Restaurant)

| Metric | Below Avg | Average | Strong | Best-in-class |
|--------|-----------|---------|--------|---------------|
| Engagement rate (Feed) | <0.5% | 0.5-1.5% | 2-4% | >5% |
| Reel watch-through | <30% | 30-50% | 50-70% | >70% |
| Story completion rate | <40% | 40-60% | 60-80% | >80% |
| Follower growth MoM | <1% | 1-3% | 3-8% | >10% |
| Profile → follow rate | <3% | 3-8% | 8-15% | >15% |
| Saves per post (÷ reach) | <0.3% | 0.3-1% | 1-3% | >3% |
| DM response time | >24h | 4-24h | <4h | <1h (badge) |

### Food/Restaurant Instagram Content Mix (recommended)
| Format | % of Content | Why |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| Reels (behind-scenes, prep, ambiance) | 40-50% | Reach non-followers |
| Carousels (menu items, event recap) | 25-30% | Saves + dwell time |
| Stories (daily updates, offers, polls) | Daily | Community + DM entry |
| Feed images (hero dishes, events) | 15-20% | Brand aesthetic |
| Lives (special events, chef sessions) | Occasional | Engagement spike |
